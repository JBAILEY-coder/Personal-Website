Personality Tool Test

Is it True?

One's personality is not stagnant, it is ever-evolving, especially daily. Personally, I think the personality test is somewhat wrong, mostly accurate but definitely wrong in some aspects. But what should we expect from an online tool that doesn't really know our personalities? This test may have been taken at a time where I was feeling low, or feeling to rig the game but personally I do have some opinions on where the test went wrong in depicting my personality. I prefer to think of myself as an ambivert rather than an introvert, I do enjoy introverted acttivities, but I also enjoy entroverted activities but I would definitely not classify myself into one single category. The intuitive part is definitely correct, but the nature section? Definitely wrong. I've realized that I am the type of person to act based upon my feelings than to think it through. or example if I am overwhelmed in in a situation I'll cry and throw down whatever it is I am doing, or cry while doing it, therefore the identity section is somewhat right as I have turbulent behavior.
