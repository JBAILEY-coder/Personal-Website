Themes

The theme for my website is something I'd like to call subtle school work

 Subtle school work in this case has a double background, that is one with a blurry background to show how blurry and fuzzy school can makes us while having a inner desk background to show the school setting. I used fonts and a monotonous color of either black, white or purple to showcase this. The fonts used were Monotype Corsiva for sub headings, Agency FB for paragraphs and Freestyle Script for major headings.
